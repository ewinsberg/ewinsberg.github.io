Books

  • Cambridge Elements: Models in Science and Decision Making

    Eric Winsberg and Stephanie Harvard. Cambridge University Press, 2024.

    Element in the Cambridge Elements series on philosophy of science and decision-making.

  • The Probability Map of the Universe: Essays on David Albert’s Time and Chance

    Barry Loewer, Brad Weslake, and Eric Winsberg, eds. Harvard University Press, 2023.

    A collection of papers on the foundations of statistical mechanics and the arrow of time.

  • Philosophy and Climate Science

    Eric Winsberg. Cambridge University Press, 2018.

    Reviewed in Notre Dame Philosophical Reviews, the British Journal for Philosophy of Science, Philosophy of Science, Journal for General Philosophy of Science, and Ethics, Policy and Environment. CHOICE Honorable Mention award, Atmospheric Science Librarians International.

  • Climate Modeling: Philosophical and Conceptual Issues

    Elisabeth S. Lloyd and Eric Winsberg, eds. Palgrave Macmillan, 2018.

    A collection of papers on the foundations of climate models. Contributors include Nancy Cartwright, Mathias Frisch, Reto Knutti, Michael Mann, Linda Mearns, Jonathan Rougier, Ben Santer, Elisabeth Lloyd, Jay Odenbaugh, Naomi Oreskes, Wendy Parker, and Eric Winsberg.

  • Science in the Age of Computer Simulation

    Eric Winsberg. University of Chicago Press, 2010.

    Short list for the 2011 Lakatos Award. Reviewed in Science, Physics Today, Metascience, Notre Dame Philosophical Reviews, Theoria, Minds and Machines. Author meets critics at 2011 Eastern APA.
